Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the search intent we see most across enterprise and growth-stage teams evaluating PjnTech.

What makes PjnTech different from other digital agencies?

We combine marketing strategists, certified performance marketers, and product-grade engineering squads under one roof. That lets us ship cross-channel experiments fast and prove impact on revenue, not vanity metrics.

How quickly can we launch an SEO or PPC sprint?

Discovery and technical audits are delivered within 5 business days. From there we prioritize quick-win fixes and launch high-impact campaigns within 14 days, while longer-term content roadmaps run in parallel.

Do you support enterprise security and compliance needs?

Yes. Our delivery pods operate with SOC 2 aligned controls, NDA coverage, GDPR-ready data handling, and region-specific compliance reviews so enterprises can move faster without adding risk.

Can you partner with in-house teams?

Absolutely. Most of our engagements embed alongside internal marketing or product teams, giving them extra firepower, technical guidance, and always-on reporting.
